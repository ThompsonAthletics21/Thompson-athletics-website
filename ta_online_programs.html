About the Coach
Grant Thompson
CPT CNC Bodybuilding Specialist Fort Myers, FL
I didn't start in a gym. I started with 15-minute workouts in my bedroom at 13 — no equipment, no coach. By high school I was training calisthenic sessions at my local park every summer, and all four years of high school I played varsity soccer.

It was a broken ankle my junior year that changed everything. Six months on the sidelines sent me to the weight room — and I never really left. Since 2020, I've spent nearly every day lifting, tracking my nutrition, and recovering.

Over the past year I've built my career around coaching others to do the same. When a client hands me their health and fitness, that's not something I take lightly. That trust keeps me learning, researching, and always progressing as a coach.
